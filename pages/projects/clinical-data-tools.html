Clinical Data Tools

Focused utilities for repeatable clinical informatics workflows.

This project covers lightweight tooling for cohort construction, data quality checks, feature definitions, missingness summaries, and transparent analytic handoffs.

The goal is not heavy platform work. It is practical tooling that reduces ambiguity and helps research teams move faster with fewer surprises.
