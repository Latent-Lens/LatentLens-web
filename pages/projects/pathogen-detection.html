Pathogen Detection Platform

This initiative is focused on developing a pathogen detection platform that could enable faster, more accurate identification of infections at the point of care, reducing reliance on time-consuming centralized clinical laboratory testing. By shortening the time from sample collection to actionable results, the platform aims to enable more rapid, precise treatment decisions and improve patient outcomes.

Deliverables

  • Deliverables are currently under development and will be defined as the platform design, validation strategy, and pathogen target lists mature.
